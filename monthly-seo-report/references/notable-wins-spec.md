# Notable Wins Specification

For each SEO client, extract 3 key wins to send to Ehtisham for review. These wins are used by the TWG marketing team in client communications and social proof.

## Format

Each win should be:
- **Short and punchy** — one line, maximum two sentences.
- **Positive only** — showcase improvements, growth, and achievements.
- **Concrete** — include specific numbers: before/after, percentage increase, absolute figures.

## Examples

Good:
- "37 calls from Google Business Profile this month — up 22% from last month"
- "14 leads from ChatGPT referrals — a new AI traffic channel"
- "Page 1 rankings increased from 32 to 45 keywords"
- "Organic traffic up 18% month-on-month (412 → 486 sessions)"
- "Website audit score improved to 94% — above the 90% target"
- "New blog post ranked #3 for 'emergency plumber Brisbane' within 2 weeks"

Bad (too vague, no numbers):
- "SEO is performing well"
- "Traffic improved"
- "Good progress this month"

## Where wins come from

Pull wins from any data source that shows positive movement:
- GSC: click or impression increases, new page 1 rankings
- GA4: organic traffic growth, new traffic channels (e.g. AI Assistant)
- GBP: call/click increases, new reviews, high engagement
- DataForSEO: keyword movements, backlink growth, audit score improvements
- AI/LLM: new appearances in AI search results

## Presentation in ClickUp

Post the 3 wins at the top of the ClickUp task description, in a clearly marked section:

```markdown
## 🏆 Notable Wins — {Client Name}
**Status:** ⏳ Pending Approval

1. {Win 1}
2. {Win 2}
3. {Win 3}

---
```

These wins need to be approved in ClickUp before being used by the marketing team. The approval mechanism (checklist item or status change) should be clear so the reviewer can approve or request changes.

## Reviewer

Ehtisham reviews the wins. Once approved, they can be forwarded to the marketing team for use in client update emails, case studies, or social media.
