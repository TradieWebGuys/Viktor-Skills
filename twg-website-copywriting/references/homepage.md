# Homepage — Structure & Rules

Read the parent `SKILL.md` and the client's `client-kb.md` first. This file covers what is unique to the homepage.

---

## Section Breakdown

| Section | Word Count |
|---|---|
| Intro / Hero | 80–120 words |
| Services Overview | 200–280 words |
| USPs / Why Choose | 80–120 words |
| Trust Signals | 80–120 words |
| Final CTA | ~80 words |
| **Total** | **~600–800 words** |

---

## Rules

- No FAQs on the homepage.
- No "Areas We Service" section unless requested by the client — check `client-kb.md`.
- Services overview items link to dedicated service pages. Use descriptive anchor text pulled from the service page slugs in `client-kb.md`, not generic "click here" or "learn more".
- The hero opening follows the reader-first pacing rule in `SKILL.md` — first sentence in plain homeowner language, technical or claim-heavy detail in sentence two or three.
- Trust signals lean on the strongest 2–3 USPs from `client-kb.md`. Include at least one citable claim (guarantee length, years experience, licence number) for AIO.
- CTA placements: hero, after the services overview, after USPs, final close. Pull CTA wording and contact details from `client-kb.md`.
