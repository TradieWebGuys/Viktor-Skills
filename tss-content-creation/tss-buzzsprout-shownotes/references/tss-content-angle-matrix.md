# Content Angle Decision Matrix — The Site Shed Podcast

Use this to determine the correct title angle for each episode before writing the YouTube title, BuzzSprout episode name, or any headline content.

---

## Step 1 — What type of episode is it?

- Guest shares a success story or case study → **go to 2A**
- Expert guest, educational, or topic-driven → **go to 2B**

---

## 2A — Success Story / Case Study

**Are they a TWG client?**

| Scenario | Angle | Example format |
|---|---|---|
| Yes + specific results + clear strategy | Results-Driven Hook | "$120k in Booked Jobs in 6 Weeks" |
| Yes + specific results + no clear strategy | Results-Focused | "What Caused the Lead Spike" |
| Yes + no specific results + clear strategy | Strategy Insight | "The CRM Tweak That Changed Everything" |
| Yes + no specific results + no clear strategy | Relatable Client Journey | "From Burnout to Better Jobs" |
| No + measurable outcome | External Case Study Hook | "How This Tradie Hit 7 Figures Solo" |
| No + story-based + business focus | Business Shift or Lesson | "The Website Fix That Improved Conversions" |
| No + story-based + personal focus | Personal or Mindset Transformation | "The Turning Point in Their Business Journey" |

---

## 2B — Topic-Based / Expert / Educational

| Dominant focus | Angle | Example format |
|---|---|---|
| Strategy or framework | Step-by-Step Insight | "How to Set Up a Lead Qualifying Workflow" |
| Opinion or myth-busting | Contrarian Take | "Why You Shouldn't Offer Free Quotes" |
| Personal philosophy or experience | Thought Leadership | "Lessons from 15 Years in Business" |
| Tool, tech, or product | Tech Breakdown or Review | "Best CRM Tools for Tradies (2025 Edition)" |
| Industry trend or big picture | Trend Perspective | "Why Tradies Are Losing to Online-First Competitors" |

---

## Output

Once the angle is identified, write the title to match the pattern, then apply the naming convention:

**[Title] | ft. [Guest Name] | Ep. [###]**

Example: `Why 60% of Business Transitions Fail (And It's Not the Tax Strategy) | ft. Andrea Carpenter | Ep. 505`
